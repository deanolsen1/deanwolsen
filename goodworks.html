Helping the Homeless

Homeless Support Services at Bethel Lutheran

I trained homeless men and women to use computers and the Internet to apply for employment, housing, and other resources. These actions led many but not all to permanent housing and jobs, breaking the cycle of poverty and homelessness.

Helping the Young

Horizons Summer Camp for Teens

For two summers, I provided counsel, advice, and good food while planning and preparing meals at this summer camp where half of the campers were from troubled homes and had behavioral challenges. I learned patience, compassion, and the power of a good meal in changing everyone&s perspectives.

Helping the Alcoholic

As a grateful, recovered alcoholic, I have helped others to attain the sobriety I now enjoy by sharing my experience, strength, and hope as a sponsor of men new to AA and by speaking to groups large and small about my successes and challenges. The power of understanding and then sharing my own history and hearing the life histories of others is one of the core concepts of my startup, LifeMapping. I'd like my legacy to be bringing this transformative practice to as wide an audience as I can.
