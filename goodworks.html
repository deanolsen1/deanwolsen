Dean Olsen

Good works.

Help to the Distraught

Stephen Ministry at Bethel Lutheran

Act as a lay counselor to congregation members going through hard times, particularly dealing with end-of-life and loss of a spouse issues. My involvement has included over 100 hours of training, twice-monthly supervision sessions, and weekly meetings with care receivers

Help to the Homeless

Homeless Support Services at Bethel Lutheran

Trained homeless clients to use computers and the Internet to apply for employment, housing, and other resources, leading to permanent housing and jobs for many to help end cycle of poverty and homelessness



Help to the Young

Horizons Summer Camp for Teens

For two summers, I provided counsel, advice, and good food while planning and preparing meals at this summer camp where half of the campers were from troubled homes and had behavioral challenges



Help to the Alcoholic

AA Sponsor & Speaker

As a grateful, recovered alcoholic, I have been blessed by opportunities to help others to attain the sobriety I now enjoy by sharing my experience, strength, and hope as a sponsor of men new to AA and by speaking to large groups about my successes and challenges



Help to the Visual Arts

Volunteer at the Madison Museum of Contemporary Art

Reinvigorated MMoCA's planned giving program with new benefits, website, and collateral by researching and recommending best practices for planned giving and annual campaigns from a broad spectrum of peer institutions

Help to the Community

Fund raising for 511 North Carroll Street

Planned, executed, and closed on a fund raising project which enabled this landmarked community organization to replace all its decrepit chairs with new ones
